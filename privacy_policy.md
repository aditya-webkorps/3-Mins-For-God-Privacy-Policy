# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 28, 2025

**Effective Date:** November 28, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to **3 Mins for God** (the "App"), a prayer and devotional application developed by TryTank Research Institute ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App. By downloading, installing, or using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. App Settings and Preferences

We collect the following information to provide you with a personalized experience:

- **Language Preference:** Your selected language (English or Spanish) to display the App interface in your preferred language
- **Theme Preference:** Your choice of light or dark theme mode
- **Notification Settings:** Your notification preferences including:
  - Whether daily notifications are enabled or disabled
  - Selected days for receiving notifications
  - Custom notification times for each day
  - Weekly reflection notification preferences

**Storage Location:** This information is stored locally on your device using the device's local storage system.

**Purpose:** These settings are used solely to provide you with a customized App experience and to deliver notifications according to your preferences.

### 2. Game Progress Data

We collect and store the following information related to your crossword puzzle gameplay:

- **Game Attempts:** Words you type while playing the daily crossword puzzle
- **Game Dates:** Dates associated with your gameplay sessions
- **Progress Data:** Your crossword puzzle progress to maintain your game state

**Storage Location:** This information is stored locally on your device in a local database.

**Purpose:** This data is used to save your game progress and allow you to continue playing where you left off. Game data is automatically cleared for dates older than the current day.

### 3. Contact Form Information (Optional)

If you choose to contact us through the App's contact form, we may collect:

- **Name:** Your name as provided by you
- **Email Address:** Your email address as provided by you
- **Message Content:** The content of your message

**Storage Location:** This information is **NOT stored** in the App. It is sent directly to our support email address using your device's default email application.

**Purpose:** This information is used solely to respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

**Note:** You are not required to provide this information. Contact form submission is entirely optional.

### 4. App Version Information

The App automatically retrieves your App version number for display purposes in the settings screen.

**Storage Location:** This information is not stored and is used for display purposes only.

**Purpose:** To show you the current version of the App you are using.

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we **do not** collect:

- ❌ **No Analytics Data:** We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any analytics data
- ❌ **No Advertising Identifiers:** We do not collect advertising IDs or any data for advertising purposes
- ❌ **No Location Data:** While the App requests location permission (for potential future features), we do not currently collect, store, or use your location information
- ❌ **No Camera Data:** While the App requests camera permission (for potential future features), we do not currently collect, store, or use any photos or camera data
- ❌ **No User Account Information:** The App does not require user registration or login. We do not collect usernames, passwords, or any account-related information
- ❌ **No Payment Information:** The App is free to use and does not collect any payment or financial information
- ❌ **No Biometric Data:** We do not collect fingerprint, face ID, or any biometric information
- ❌ **No Contacts:** We do not access or collect information from your device's contact list
- ❌ **No Device Identifiers:** We do not collect device IDs, IP addresses, or other device identifiers for tracking purposes

## How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

### 1. App Functionality

- To remember your language preference and display the App in your chosen language
- To maintain your theme preference (light or dark mode)
- To save and restore your game progress in crossword puzzles
- To deliver local notifications according to your preferences
- To display the App version number

### 2. Customer Support

- To respond to your inquiries when you contact us through the contact form
- To provide technical support and assistance

### 3. App Improvement

- We may use anonymized, aggregated data to understand general App usage patterns (without identifying individual users)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All user preferences and game progress data are stored **locally on your device**. This means:

- Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- We do not have access to your locally stored data
- Your data is protected by your device's security measures
- You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App

### Data Retention

- **App Settings:** Retained until you change them or uninstall the App
- **Game Progress:** Daily crossword puzzle data is automatically cleared after the current day
- **Contact Form Data:** Not stored in the App; handled by your email provider

### Security Measures

While we store data locally on your device, we implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- Data is stored using secure local storage mechanisms provided by the operating system
- No sensitive data is transmitted over the internet without encryption
- The App uses secure connections (HTTPS) when downloading audio files

**Note:** While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your privacy to the best of our ability.

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase Storage (Google)

We use Firebase Storage, a service provided by Google, to:

- **Purpose:** Download audio devotional files to your device
- **Data Transmitted:** No personal user data is transmitted. Only audio file downloads are requested.
- **User Data Collected:** Firebase Storage does not collect any personal information from our App users.
- **Privacy Policy:** Google's Privacy Policy applies to Firebase services. You can review it at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Important:** Firebase Analytics and Firebase Ads are **disabled** in our App configuration. We do not use any analytics or advertising features provided by Firebase.

### Email Service

When you use the contact form, the App uses your device's default email application to send messages:

- **Data Transmitted:** Only the information you provide in the contact form (name, email, message)
- **Storage:** Your email provider's privacy policy governs how this data is handled
- **Our Access:** We only receive emails sent to our support address
